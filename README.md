### 🙋 About Me

I'm an **Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering** student at **RVCE**, blending hardware engineering with software architectures, deep math, and data-driven exploration.

- 🔭 **Leadership** — Serving as President of **Dhruva**, RVCE's Astronomy & Astrophysics club, overseeing data, radio, and research verticals.
- 📡 **Radio Astronomy** — Currently designing, simulating, and building pyramidal horn antennas to capture the 21 cm neutral hydrogen line ($1420.4\text{ MHz}$).
