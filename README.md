### 🙋 About Me

I am an **Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering** student at **RVCE**, working at the intersection of embedded hardware architectures, computational astrophysics, and low-level digital security.

- 🔭 **Leadership** — Serving as President of **Dhruva**, RVCE's Astronomy & Astrophysics club, guiding our data, radio, and research verticals.
- 📡 **Radio Astronomy** — Leading the team designing, simulating, and building a pyramidal horn antenna from $2\text{mm}$ aluminum sheets to map the 21 cm neutral hydrogen line at $1420.4\text{ MHz}$.
- 🔐 **Cybersecurity** — Active CTF competitor with a deep focus on cryptography, blockchain security exploits, and digital forensics.
- ⚡ **Fun Fact** — My system stability formula: **Debugging = $40\%$ SVD/Linear Algebra + $30\%$ Tcl scripting + $30\%$ hoping the hardware matches the simulation.**
